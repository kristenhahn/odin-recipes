Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Makes 1 sandwich

Cheap, tasty, fast, and filling. But do you really need instructions for this? Here they are, just in case.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • Peanut butter to taste
  • Jelly to taste

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slidce of bread.
  2. Spread jelly on top of the peanut butter.
  3. Top sandwich with the remaining slice of bread.
  4. Cut sandwich in half and serve on a plate if you're feeling fancy.
