Almond Flour Chocolate Cupcakes

Description

These cupcakes are tasty, fast, and easy. They're rich and chocolaty without being overly sweet. They can even be kicked up a notch with some cayenne pepper if you enjoy spice

They're healthier and lower in carbs than most desserts. They're also gluten free, and (if you choose) nut free!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 Tbsp maple syrup or agave syrup
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup pecans/walnuts - chopped (optional)
